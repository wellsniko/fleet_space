FleetSpace

Your job is to dock the superlaser onto the Death Star. This laser will be used to take out the planet Alderaan, home to a massive force of the Rebellion.
This Death Star landing simulator will familiarize you with the controls of an actual Imperial Bomber from from Star Wars. Successful docking is achieved when your location on the x, y, and z axis are within 10m of the landing zone, all rotation angles are within 0.2 °, and your velocity is less than 3 m/s. All value fields on the interface should be blue. Movement speed should be slow and requires patience, accuracy, and precision.

W - Increase up speed
S - Increase down speed
A - Increase left strafe speed
D - Increase right strafe speed
R - Increase forward speed
F - Increase backward speed
Q - Increase roll left speed
E - Increase roll right speed
▲ - Pitch Up
◀ - Yaw left
▼ - Pitch Down
▶ - Yaw Right
