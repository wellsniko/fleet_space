FleetSpace

This Death Star landing simulator will familiarize you with the controls of the actual Star Destroyer from Star Wars. Successful docking is achieved when all numbers on the interface are under 0.2. Movement speed should be slow and requires patience, accuracy, and precision.


W - increase up speed
S - increase down speed
A - Increase left strafe speed
D - increase right strafe speed
R - increase forward speed
F - increase backward speed
Q - increase roll left speed
E - increase roll right speed
Up Arrow - Pitch Up
Down Arrow - Pitch Down
Left Arrow - Yaw left
Right Arrow - Yaw Right
